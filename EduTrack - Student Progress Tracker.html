Main Features

EduTrack is designed to make student progress management seamless and motivating. Explore the core functionalities that will help you achieve your academic goals efficiently.

Assignment Submissions with Deadlines

Submit assignments on time and keep track of upcoming deadlines with automated reminders and status updates.

Visual Analytics of Grades and Progress

Understand your academic performance better with detailed charts and progress reports tailored to your courses.

Feedback Loops Between Teacher and Student

Receive personalized feedback and communicate efficiently with teachers to improve your learning experience.

Classroom Notifications

Stay updated with real-time notifications about class announcements, events, and important deadlines.

Study Goal Planner

Set achievable study goals, manage your schedule, and stay motivated with built-in goal tracking tools.
